# EasyTk

**Save public videos to your Photos library with ease.**

A privacy-first iOS video saving utility with a clean, modern dark interface.

## Features

- **One-Tap Saving** - Paste a link, tap save, done
- **Privacy-First** - Videos auto-delete from servers in ~4 minutes
- **Clean Design** - Premium Mint × Carbon dark theme
- **No Account Required** - Just download and use
- **Download History** - Track your saved videos
- **Points & Rewards** - Earn bonus downloads

## How It Works

1. Copy a public video link
2. Paste it in EasyTk
3. Confirm you have permission
4. Tap "Save to Photos"
5. Video saved to your library!

## Privacy

Your privacy matters. EasyTk:
- Temporarily processes videos on secure servers
- Automatically deletes all cloud copies within ~4 minutes
- Never stores your videos permanently
- Doesn't track your downloads
