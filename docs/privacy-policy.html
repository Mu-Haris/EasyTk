Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

Muhammad Haris ("we," "us," or "our") operates the EasyTk mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA, when you consent)
  • Advertising interaction data

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification: Names, emails, phone numbers
  • No location data: We don't access your location
  • No contacts: We don't access your contacts
  • No browsing history: We don't track your browsing
  • No video tracking: We don't track which videos you save
  • No photos access: We only write to Photos, never read

Video Processing

When you use EasyTk to save a video:

  • The video link you provide is sent to our secure servers
  • The video is temporarily processed for download
  • The video is transmitted to your device
  • All server-side copies are automatically deleted within approximately 4 minutes

We do not retain copies of videos beyond this temporary processing window.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Download history
  • Points and activity data
  • App preferences

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (can be reset in device settings)
  • General ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Tracking.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Automatic deletion of processed videos
  • No persistent storage of user data on servers

Children's Privacy

EasyTk is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, delete, or export. Your local device data (history, points) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
