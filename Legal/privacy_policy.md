# EasyTk Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

EasyTk ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our video saving application.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

EasyTk is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT:

- Collect personal identification information
- Track which videos you download
- Store your download history on our servers
- Require account creation or login
- Use analytics that identify individual users
- Sell or share data with third parties

## Information Processing

### Video Processing
When you use EasyTk to save a video:

1. The video link you provide is sent to our secure servers
2. The video is temporarily processed for download
3. The video is transmitted to your device
4. **All server-side copies are automatically deleted within approximately 4 minutes**

We do not retain copies of videos beyond this temporary processing window.

### Device Storage
- Download history is stored locally on your device only
- Points and activity data are stored locally on your device only
- This data never leaves your device

## Permissions

### Photos Library Access
EasyTk requests access to your Photos library solely to save videos you choose to download. We do not access, read, or upload any existing photos or videos from your library.

### Network Access
Network access is required to:
- Fetch video content from source URLs
- Process videos on our secure servers
- Download processed videos to your device

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising
EasyTk uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's privacy policy. This is limited to:
- Device advertising identifier (can be reset in device settings)
- General ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

### No Other Third Parties
We do not integrate with any other third-party services that collect user data.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Automatic deletion of processed videos
- No persistent storage of user data on servers

## Children's Privacy

EasyTk is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to:
- Access
- Correct
- Delete
- Export

Your local device data (history, points) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
